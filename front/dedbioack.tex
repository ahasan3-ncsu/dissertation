\chapter*{BIOGRAPHY}

ATM Jahid Hasan was born in Dhaka, Bangladesh.
He also spent the first 25 years of his life there.
ATM Jahid received his Bachelor of Science degree in Nuclear Engineering
in 2019 from the University of Dhaka.
In 2021, he moved to the US to attend graduate school.
So far, he has received a Graduate Cerificate in Materials Informatics
and a Master of Nuclear Engineering degree
from the North Carolina State University.
His research has focused on atomistic simulations
and other computational methods.
He likes to solve problems and contributes to open-source software.
